## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of `GestureNav` seriously. If you have found a vulnerability, please do **NOT** open a public issue.

### Instructions

1.  **Email:** Please send an email to the [repository owner](https://github.com/lolbanoori) at *banoorizohair@gmail.com*.
2.  **Details:** Include as much information as possible:
    *   Version impacted.
    *   Steps to reproduce.
    *   Potential impact.
3.  **Response:** We will aim to acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide a timeline for a fix.

Thank you for helping keep the community safe!
